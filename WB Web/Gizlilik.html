Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 22, 2026

At Wolfblood, we value your privacy. As a game played via the Telegram messaging platform, we are committed to processing your data transparently and securely. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with the Wolfblood bot (@wolfbloodbot), we collect specific data necessary to facilitate the gameplay experience. This information falls into two categories:

A. Telegram Public Information:
  • Telegram User ID: A unique numerical identifier provided by Telegram.
  • Username / First Name: To display your name in game lobbies and death messages.
  • Profile Picture URL: Used temporarily to generate game avatars or "wanted" posters within the game.
B. Game Data:
  • Game Stats: Wins, losses, roles played, and ranking points (MMR).
  • Inventory: Items owned (Mints, Tokens, Tickets) and equipped cosmetics.
  • Group Activity: Logs of games played in specific Telegram groups for leaderboard calculation.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for the functionality of the game:

  • To manage game sessions, track roles, and determine winners.
  • To maintain global and local leaderboards (Ranked Mode).
  • To prevent cheating and multi-accounting (Duplicate Account Detection).
  • To enforce our Community Guidelines (e.g., detecting spam or automated abuse).

3. Data Retention & Moderation

We do not store private chat messages. However, gameplay logs (commands sent to the bot, actions taken during the night phase) are stored to ensure fair play.

Global Bans: If a user violates our Terms of Service (e.g., hate speech, botting), their Telegram User ID is added to a blocklist shared across all Wolfblood instances. This is necessary to protect the community.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Data is processed solely on our secure servers.

Note: Since Wolfblood operates on Telegram, your interactions are also subject to Telegram's own Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights (GDPR & Deletion)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you (use the /mydata command).
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and game stats. This action is irreversible.

To request data deletion, please contact our support team or use the /deleteaccount command within the bot (requires confirmation).

6. Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Database access is restricted to authorized senior developers only.

Have Questions?

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us via our support group.
