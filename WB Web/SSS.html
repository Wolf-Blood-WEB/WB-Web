Frequently Asked Questions

🛠️ Setup and Settings

How do I add the bot to my group?

To add the bot to your group, find the user @wolfbloodbot on Telegram and invite it via the "Add Member" section. It is strongly recommended to grant Administrator rights (delete messages and pin messages permissions are required) for the bot to function correctly.

How do I change the group language?

You must be an administrator to change the language in your group. Type /setlang in the chat and select one of the available languages (English, Turkish, Persian, etc.) from the list.

How do I change the bot's private message language?

To change the language the bot uses in private messages (PM) with you, go to the bot's private chat and use the /setlang command. This setting only affects you.

Where can I access official support groups?

To list active game groups, you can type /grouplist in the bot's private chat or any group. You can also access our official community from the "Groups" tab on our homepage.

🎮 Game Mechanics

How to play?

Wolfblood features the struggle between villagers and werewolves. The game consists of night and day cycles. For a detailed guide, check the Gameplay page on our website. To start the game, use the /startgame command in the group.

How do I activate Ranked mode?

Ranked mode is where game results affect the global leaderboard. To enable this mode in your group, use the /setmode command and select "Ranked" from the list.

How do I access the role list?

To see descriptions of all roles in the game, type /roles in the bot's private chat. Detailed filtering options are also available on our Roles page.

What is the "Claim" command and what does it do?

The claim command allows players to list their claimed roles in a single list during the game. For example: if you type /claim Seer, the bot lists you as "claiming Seer". This list is important for strategy and is automatically cleared when the game ends.

💰 Economy and Items

What are the items (Mint, Ticket, Token) used for?

There are 3 basic units in the Wolfblood economy:

  • Mint (🌿): The basic in-game currency. Earned by winning games. Used to buy items from the market.
  • Ticket (🎫): Used to enter special events or tournaments.
  • Token (🪙): A valuable unit used to buy rare and special collectible items.
Can personal items be purchased in the bot?

Yes! With the Mints you earn, you can buy the following from the in-game market:

  • Player Tag: Special titles that appear next to your name.
  • GIF/Sticker Packs: Special visuals you can use during the game.
  • Emoji Sets: Special emojis displayed in death or lynch messages.

⚖️ Management and Rules

What are the global rules?

The rules applicable in all groups where Wolfblood is played are:

  • Spamming and locking the game is prohibited.
  • Racist, sexist, and hate speech are strictly prohibited (Cause for Global Ban).
  • It is forbidden to prove roles by sending screenshots outside the game (PM).
Can we have our own group rules?

Yes. As long as they do not violate global rules (e.g., hate speech cannot be allowed), every group is free to determine its own game order and chat rules.

Can sanctions be applied to troll players in the group?

Yes. Group admins can use the /gameban command for players who sabotage the game. This command prevents the specified player from joining games in that group for a certain period but does not kick them from the group.

Why can't I join games? (Am I banned?)

If you cannot join a game, it might be one of the following:

  • Global Ban: You cannot play in any group where the bot is present. Given for serious rule violations.
  • Game Ban: You are banned only from games in that specific group.
  • Cooldown: If you fled too many games (flee), you might be temporarily penalized.

You can write to our support group to find out your status.
