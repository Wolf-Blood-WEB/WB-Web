Wolfblood bot was developed in May 2022 and was mainly inspired by @werewolfbot, a fantastic creation by Para, the Father of the Telegram Werewolf Game and the original developer of the bot. We will be forever grateful to Para for inventing and coding this game.

🤝 We would also like to thank Para and the entire GreyWolf Development Team for keeping the original bot running since 2016.

New Game Modes

Love Mode: Are you ready for some romance? In this mode, every player is in love. On the first night, your lover will be chosen, and you'll find out who they are the next day. This mode is exclusive to games with an even number of players (e.g., 6, 8, 10).

Simplified Custom Mode: Starting a custom game is now easier than ever. You can simply specify the number of roles you want to add, and the bot will do the rest. For example, instead of typing /startcustom 🔪🔪🔪🔪🔪🔪🔪🔪🔪🔪, you can now just use /startcustom 9🔪. This feature currently works for adding up to 9 of each role at a time.

Treasure Mode: This mode is a spin on the standard ranked game with an enhanced point system that scales with the number of players. You'll earn more points for your victories and get a chance to win mystery boxes. These boxes contain temporary rewards like Player Tags, Temporary Emojis, ForceStart Tokens, and Jersey Numbers. At the end of the game, a few players are randomly selected to receive one of these prizes for 3 to 7 days.

Wolfblood Store 🛍️🛒

The Wolfblood Store is where players can enhance their gaming experience with various in-game items. The primary currency is WB Mint (🪙), earned simply by playing.

Store Items:

◽️ Wolf Blood Currency (WB Mint): How to earn Mint? Simply by playing a game. It will be given to players randomly by the bot based on the number of players in the game.

  • 1 🪙 = 10-19 players
  • 2 🪙 = 29 players
  • ... and so on up to 10 🪙 at 100 players.

◽️ Emoji Fragments (🧩): Fragments enable players to create custom emojis. The emoji store has been updated to offer both temporary (1 week, 1 month, 1 year) and permanent emojis.

◽️ Role Vouchers (🪪): These encourage players to try out different roles and diversify their gameplay. Prices vary per role (Basic, Advance, Expert, and Pro).

◽️ FS (ForceStart) Tokens (⭐️): Allow players to /forcestart a game without being an admin. You can purchase 1 FS Token for 5 Mints.

◽️ Gifting & Transfer Fees (🔂): The fee for the /transfer command has been set to 5 Mints. This command can be used only once a day, while /gift has unlimited usage.

✅ Telegram Stars (⭐️): You can now use Telegram Stars to purchase a wide range of items, including temporary emojis, custom GIFs, Mints, custom messages, and role vouchers.

Commands:

  • ▪️/profile - Here you can see a list of all items available in your WB Wallet.
  • ▪️/store - Use this command to buy items from our store.

Wolfblood Items ✨

Discover unique items that give you a strategic advantage in the game.

🌱 Fake Name Ticket [🎭]: Use the /fakename command to set a fake name that the bot will use throughout the game. Confuse your opponents and gain an edge!

🌱 ForceJoin Token [💪]: With this token, you can force another player to join the game.

🌱 Bonus Points Ticket [🎰]: Activate this ticket to multiply your points by x2, helping you level up your gameplay.

🌱 Fake Role Ticket [🔖]: Use the /fakerole command to set a fake role. Once activated, your role will appear as the fake role, hiding your real one for a single game. Get ready to have some fun and confuse everyone!

Donations 🎁

We are grateful for all donations that help support the bot's development. Donors receive special features to show our appreciation.

Donation Features:

✔️ Player Tags: Donors can now set a custom 3-letter tag to display their VIP status in the community. For example: [PRO] Wolf Blood.

✔️ Emoji Identifiers: Donors receive an updated emoji based on their donation amount:

  • 💵 $5 - $49
  • 💶 $50 - $99
  • 💷 $100 and above

✔️ Special Message [📝]: With a donation level above $50, you can use the /specialmessage command to set a custom message that appears randomly at the end of the game if you win.

✔️ Custom GIFs: Donors who contribute $10 or more can set custom GIFs to be displayed in-game.
