🪽 The Fallen Angel

Ability: Bring back a dead player, but at the sacrifice of another player of your choosing.

Condition: This can only happen once per game.

×

🕯️ The Virgin

Ability: When killed in the night, choose another player to sacrifice themselves to keep you alive.

Condition: This can only happen once.

×

💃 Damsel in Distress

Ability: When in danger of being eliminated, the Damsel becomes desperate and picks two players that must switch roles immediately.

×

💋 The Flirt

Ability: Picks one player per night and distracts them from using their ability.

Condition: Each player can only be flirted with once per game.

×

🌿 Mother Nature

Ability: Is aware of who has the Seer role. They must protect that player at all costs.

Condition: Must sacrifice themselves after a successful vote has been placed on the Seer to keep them in the game.

×

🎭 Shape Shifter / Faceless

Ability: Can take the form of any one role during the night. This becomes your identity for the game but is powerless.

Strategic Use: Helpful for the Werewolf team to confuse Villagers, forcing them to choose between two people claiming the same role.

Note: The card artwork looks like a Villager, but it’s an illusion.

×

🧚‍♀️ The Fairy / Sandman

Ability: Sprinkles fairy dust (or sand) on a player in the night. That player sleeps in and cannot talk during the next day phase.

×

📖 The Storyteller / Vision

Ability: The ability alters the moderator's story for one round.

Note: A unique mechanic that changes the narrative flow of the game.

×

✨ The Miracle

Ability: If eliminated first, becomes reborn as soon as a Villager role vote passes to be burned.

×

🦠 The Infected

Start: Starts off as a Villager.

Trigger: If the Werewolves pick you in the night, you become a Werewolf and replace another wolf of your choice. The selected werewolf dies.

×

🕶️ Hitman

Setup: Two Hitmen exist (similar to Masons).

Objective: Each Hitman has to outlast the other. The last Hitman standing must continue playing until all Werewolves are revealed.

×

🐣 Rebirth

Ability: If killed, comes back as a random character from the characters who have already been killed.
