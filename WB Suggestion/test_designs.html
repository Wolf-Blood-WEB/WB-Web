Bio-Soldier

Experimental Design

(VILLAGE)

You're a top soldier. A new version, like version 2.0 of the peasant. Every night you will visit someone. If the player you visit has any kind of homicidal tendencies, you will self-destruct them and everyone who is there at that moment will be unrecognizable. At the same time, if someone tries to kill someone you visit, you will also self-destruct. If you go to the target of a serial killer, he won't die, but he will be injured by the explosion and won't be able to attack the next night. If the Wolves and the Serial Killer attack you, they will kill you.

Cult Rate: 0 [💀]

(SOLO)

You're a top soldier. A new version, like version 2.0 of the peasant. Every night you will visit someone and you will cause of an explosion and everyone who is there at that moment will be unrecognizable. At the same time, if someone tries to kill someone you visit, you will also cause an explosion. If you go to the target of a serial killer or they visit you, there might be winner but also can be drawn too. Under the drawn condition you both can not attack next night because of an injury. Against the werewolves, it depends on the pack number.

Cult Rate: 0 [💀]

Wolf Blood

Experimental Design

You are the original wolf, superior to the alpha and even the first werewolf to give him the curse of infection! You wander apart from the pack and you can't attack every night because you're a very old wolf. If you attack, there is nothing to stop you. On the first try you will kill the wise elder, the serial killer, the arsonist, even the cursed. If you have visitors, they will either die or be infected and turn into werewolves the next day. If they lynch you, you will cause someone else to die. If the soldier lynches you to save someone else, you will die.

Cult Rate: 0 [🐺]

Dealbreaker

Experimental Design

He is no different from an ordinary villager, but if he tries to be killed in any way, he will reveal the role of a random player and he will survive. If a villager tries to kill him, he will reveal someone's role to the person trying to kill him and will survive thanks to this deal. No deal if he is lynched. He will do the same thing to survive the wolves. This can only happen once, so the second time he risks being killed he will die. The serial killer will kill him outright.

Cult Rate: 50%
×

Fate bound Elder

Experimental Design

A mystical village elder protected by fate itself. When attacked, the outcome is determined by luck:
• Against Werewolf:
60% survive, 30% retaliate, 10% die
• Against Serial Killer:
50% survive, 30% retaliate, 20% die
• Against Arsonist (burn night):
50% survive, 50% die

This role is a defensive tank with the rare ability to punish killers—but fate is a double-edged sword. Investigators will always see you as "mystic but powerless," making trust a luxury.

Cult Rate: 30%
×

False Face

Experimental Design

A deceptive and manipulative entity that thrives on confusion. Every night, it marks one player:
That target appears as False Face to all vision roles (Seer, Sorcerer, etc.)
When a new target is marked, the previous one dies immediately.
All actions (investigations, protections, attacks) targeting False Face are redirected randomly—including possibly back to the source.

This role has no direct kill ability but eliminates indirectly and causes chaos among vision and power roles.

Cult Rate: 0
×

Sismolog

Experimental Design

You are a scientist specializing in earthquakes and earth sciences. Your belief in science makes your role independent of all the players. In case of lynching, there will be a big earthquake in the village! Living players:

↳25% will die under rubble due to earthquake
↳25% will be trapped under the rubble and unable to act for 1 day
↳The 25% will only feel the tremor and realize it
↳The remaining 25% will be safe and sound and will feel nothing.

Experimental Design
