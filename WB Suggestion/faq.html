📂 Submissions & Ideas

How do I get my role idea into the game?

Simple. Go to the Home page and fill out the "Suggest a Role" form. If the Alpha Developers like it, it will be added to the "Test Designs" page for the community to vote on.

I submitted an idea, but I don't see it. Why?

The wolves are picky. We receive hundreds of ideas. If your idea is too similar to an existing role, breaks the game balance, or isn't detailed enough, it might be discarded. Check the Graveyard (Removed) page just in case.

What happens if two people submit the same idea?

Great minds think alike. We usually credit the first person who submitted it, or we may combine elements from both suggestions into one ultimate role.

⚙️ Game Mechanics

What in the dark arts is "Cult Rate"?

What is the Cult Rate?
The Cult Rate is a metric that determines the relative strength, weakness, or faith level of each specific role within the game. We strive for balance; however, if you believe the current rates are unfair or could be improved, you are encouraged to submit your suggestions for adjustments to ensure a more balanced gameplay experience.

Are the "Test Designs" balanced?

Not always! That's why they are in testing. Use them at your own risk. If a role is too overpowered (OP), the community will vote to nerf it in the next patch.

What is the "Evo Mod"?

What is Evo Mod?
Evo Mod (Evolution Modification) is an advanced game mode designed for those who thrive in chaos. In this mode, roles evolve into significantly more powerful, unpredictable, and unbalanced versions of themselves. It is specifically crafted for experienced players seeking a high stakes, high-impact gameplay experience. For a full breakdown of these chaotic evolutions, visit the Evo Mod page.
