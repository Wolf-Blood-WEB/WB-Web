EVO MOD

🧬 They grow. They change. They ruin everything. 🔥

Forget balance. Forget mercy.

EVO MOD is about upgrading classic roles into monstrous versions of themselves.
This is for chaotic thrill-seekers who want to watch their game spiral into beautiful madness.

Base Form

🍻
The Drunk

Congrats! You're the village drunk! (and really, everyone knows it...). Usually just a villager, but if Wolves eat you, they miss their kill the next night.

MUTATION DETECTED

EVO Drunk

🍻

You were simply a drunked villager, only affects werewolf. But now you will also affect your visitors.

• Cultist Hunter 💂, Harlot 💋, Guardian Angel 👼, Chemist 🧪, Pawn ♟, Cultist 👤
The roles will get drunked and can not perform any action next night. If there are more than 1 cult, one of them will get drunked (newest member) but the other(s) will convert you.
