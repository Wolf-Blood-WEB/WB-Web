Snitch

❌ Old Version

The Snitch is a simple villager. It can choose a player during the first night and has the ability to reveal the role of the player to everyone if the snitch dies. But if the chosen player dies before the snitch, it will only be a simple villager.

Cult Conversion Chance: 100%

Note: It is not a 120 second role, and can appear in an 8 player game.

✨ New Version

Snitch is the nosiest resident in the entire village. His passion for gossip has led him to learn everyone’s hidden truths. On his final breath, snitch can make any player reveal their identity by snitching on their deepest darkest secret. Additionally, if the wolves dare to attack Snitch, there’s a 25% chance that one wolf’s identity gets revealed.

Traveler

❌ Old Version

Traveler : A traveler is a peasant who loves to travel. During his travels, he discovers a merchant and the merchant offers him the remaining 4 roles. The traveler chooses which role he wants to take and transforms into that role. If he doesn't like the roles, he can continue playing as a traveler.

Cult conversion chance: 60%
✨ New Version

You are on the road at the day time. You will visit players and you will make them an offer about if they want to change their roles against your offer. If they accept, you can sold that role another player. If they not, you will continue the searching.
Note: Players can choose bad roles too, only thing is you are not able to know what did you offer.

Time Lord
