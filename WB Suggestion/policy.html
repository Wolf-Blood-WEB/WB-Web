Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 24, 2026

Your secrets are safe with the pack... mostly.

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Wolf Blood Workshop. This is a community-run fan project dedicated to the evolution of the game. We value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy, even though we are just a bunch of wolves playing games.

2. Information We Collect

We believe in minimalism. We do not require you to create an account to view the site. However, we collect specific data when you interact with our tools:

  • Role Suggestions: When you submit a role idea via our forms, the content of that suggestion (Role Name, Ability, Description) becomes public community data. Do not include personal information (names, emails, phone numbers) in these fields.
  • Usage Data: We may use standard web analytics to see which pages (e.g., "Graveyard" vs "Test Designs") are most popular.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

We use local storage technology (similar to cookies) to enhance your experience. This is strictly functional:

  • Theme Preferences: Saving your preference for Dark Mode (which is the only mode, let's be honest).
  • Read States: Remembering which "New" roles you have already clicked on so we don't annoy you with notifications.

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser cache.

4. Third-Party Links

Our workshop contains links to external platforms, primarily Telegram. Once you click these links, you leave our territory. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of Telegram or any other third-party sites.

5. Data Rights

Since we do not store personal accounts, we cannot "delete your account." However, if you accidentally submitted personal information in a Role Suggestion form, please contact us via our Telegram community immediately, and an Admin will scrub the data manually.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or want to report a privacy concern, summon the admins in our Telegram group.
